John Cooling Profile

In the Beginning

Started programming in 1974. Yes, that's fifty years programming experince!

First coded banking applications using IBM Assempler. Writing the programs out long hand on coding sheets. Coding sheets turned into punch cards by enormous data preparation teams, over fifty people. Cards feed into a IBM 370 computer to assemble, link, and execute.

Bugs were corrected by mentally assembling the code fix into machine code and manually hand puching it into a REM card for the "link deck" to rerun. With this method you saved so much time, days, getting a rerun without having to reassemble the whole program.

Software Developer

Development of Banking applictions for Austratlias largest bank, Commbank.

Analyst programmer and project management of banking applications for one of Australias smallest banks, The Bank of Queensland

Analyst programmer of ERP applications for Australias largest software company, Mincom

Corporate Executive

Director and Vice President of consulting and customer value for Mincom.

Created the fastest growing and most profitable consulting pactice in the company.

Customers were global and industries were mining, utility, transport and government enterprises.

These were great roles working with staff, customers and consultants to help people achive the best they could from a top tier ERP system.

Business Owner

2006 launched SmartWorker, an IT consulting and contracting business in Australia.

Customers ranged from small software houses to Australias largest mining companies.

Consultants are mainly Australian based. Mining companies introduced overseas porjects in New Cladonia, Kasicstan, Sierra Leone, Botswan to name a few of the more exotic ones.

Entrepreneur

Buliding on the success of SmartWorker a disruptive software concept incorporationg AI and information visualisation was developed in 2014. The Syft Platform was launced in 2015. Its an innovative recruitement application. Using AI technology to disassemble job requirements and candidate profiles. Using visualisation technology to present jobs and candidates in easy to compare formats. The app runs on the latest web site technology and is back ended by either Salesforce or Workday(next released).

Patent applied for the innovative use of AI and information visualisation in the talent matching and fulfilment process.
